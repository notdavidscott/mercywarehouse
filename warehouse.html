Donation Guide


How to Donate

Donating to the Mercy Warehouse is quick, easy, and you'll be greatly blessed! Your donations are tax-deductable, and are used to provide food and water to those in need. Have some items that you wish to donate, but don't know how much they are worth? We use a top-of-the-line value determination site from the Salvation Army. Just click the link here: What is my stuff worth?
