Want to Volunteer?

We welcome businesses to come serve at Mercy Warehouse. Sorting, organizing, moving, hanging, smiling..If you wish to volunteer at the Mercy Warehouse, we've got a job or you! There is no group too small or too large to serve. Volunteering at the Mercy Warehouse is an effective way to provide employees with meaningful opportunities, engage and develop skills away from the office, break out of 'comfort zones', and boost public image.

"92% of surveyed corporate human resources executives agree that contributing business skills and expertise to a nonprofit can be an effective way to emprove employees' leadership and broader professional skill sets."
